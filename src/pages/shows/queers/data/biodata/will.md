Will has been involved in theatre behind the scenes since primary school and has worked with a wide range of amateur companies since arriving in Oxford from Melbourne. His main interest is lighting, most recently designing _After Edward_ (OTG 2022) and _Company_ (MAC 2022) as well as building previsualizations for _Nell Gwynn_ (OTG 2023). He has also called musicals including _Evita_ (OXOPS 2023) as Deputy Stage Manager, been a part of the crew for many more shows and is a member of the Oxford Theatre Guild committee.
